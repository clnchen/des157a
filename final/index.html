How to Play

1. Choose one person to be player 1, and another to be player 2
2. The strength of your attacks is decided by the sum of the dice
3. If you roll a 1, it becomes the next player's turn
4. If both dice are 1s, your opponent gets healed to full health
5. The game ends once either player loses all their health
